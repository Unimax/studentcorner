NIT DURGAPUR.

National Service Scheme or NSS, Durgapur, is one of the most burgeoning organizations of NIT.
Established in 1973, this association has rendered preiceless service to the society.
With their tenaceous conviction to the motto "Not Me but You", the members have extended their reach of mercy to every direction possible.
With their determination, there is hope that this organization will go a long way into changing the face of the society.
