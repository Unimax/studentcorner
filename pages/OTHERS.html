ATHLETICS

NIT DURGAPUR.

There are many facilities for the students to engage themselves in extra-curricular activities. Sports have become part and parcel of the campus life.
The Institute has a full fledged Physical Training Department for the students.
Students are trained in various sports and games by well experienced and qualified coaches arranged by the Department of Physical Training. There are courts for Volleyball and Basketball.
Besides the indoor game facilities available in the student's hostels, there stands a beautiful architectural Student Activity Centre,
housed in which are an Indoor Auditorium and a Gymnasium with latest equipments. The Auditorium provides Table-tennis and Badminton facilities.
The students run many hobby clubs like Photography club, Music club, Science club, Debate
