GLUG

NIT DURGAPUR.

Established in 2004 and christened as LUG (Linux Users' Group), by some of the most brilliant technocrats of NITD.
With the undaunting will of its members GLUG is continuosly striding towards its ultimate goal of popularizing GNU/Linux
and proving it better than other proprietary operating systems available. It also encourages the use of FOSS and contributions to it.
It is also a support center of GNU/Linux both inside and outside the delimition of NITD.
