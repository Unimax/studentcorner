Mathematics is, undoubtedly, the most ancient yet most intriguing of all sciences ever developed by humans.
Even in today's brutally competitive world, where economies fall like stash of dominoes,
mathematics retains its significance as a pre-requisite to being successful,
be it in corporate world or financially ascetic but astute technocratic circles. Established in 2004, the Maths 'N' Tech Club was conceived with the objective of providing a stage for instilling
in students a passion for mathematics by underlining its merit
and applicability through a plethora brainy challenges.

Activities
ANK, the Knowledge fest of NIT Durgapur, is the premier fest of academic session,
evolving out of a will of the organizers to establish once and for all that mathematics is fun.

Anveshan is an attempt by Maths 'N' Tech Club to enhance the technology quotient of its readers,
with articles contributed by students and faculty of the institute.
